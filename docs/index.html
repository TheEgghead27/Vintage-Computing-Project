About vintage computing

Before the modern days of the Internet and sleek anti-aliased designs, there were two-colored terminals (black and white or black and green!) and pixelated bitmaps. This site aims to describe some of the personal computing technologies of the past.

This site is still in early development, and the final result will look very different.
